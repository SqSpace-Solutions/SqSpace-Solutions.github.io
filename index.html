Scientific Manuscript Editing & Experimental Strategy Feedback

I provide in-depth scientific editing for research manuscripts, and scientific reports in the life sciences. I help clarify hypotheses, strengthen logic, ensure methodological transparency, and align conclusions with the presented evidence, while preserving the author’s scientific voice. I also offer constructive, non-directive feedback on experimental gaps, controls, and next-step strategies to enhance the overall impact and credibility of the work.

Laboratory Setup & Optimization Coaching for Clinical and Startup Environments

I coach clinical laboratories and biotech startups in the planning, setup, and optimization of laboratory spaces, from early-stage design to operational readiness. This includes guidance on workflow layout, equipment selection, biosafety considerations, sample handling pipelines, and scalability planning. Drawing on hands-on academic and applied lab experience, I help teams build efficient, compliant, and future-ready laboratory environments that support reproducible science, regulatory expectations, and organizational growth.

Scientific Content Creation & LLM Training and Editing

Leveraging deep subject-matter expertise in biology, I help ensure that AI systems are trained on reliable, well-structured, and context-aware scientific information suitable for research, clinical, and translational applications.
